A LITTLE BIT ABOUT ME

Hi. I’m Marya. Born in Brazil, I’ve now lived in the Bay Area since 2012. I am a Health Coach Certified through ACE and Personal trainer certified through NASM, and my passion for fitness started several years ago, when I started training Muay Thai. Since then, I am always looking for some new exercises, new classes or just having fun exercising with my husband and friends. My main goal as a coach and personal trainer is to change people’s lives by improving their quality of life working with them to achieve the long terms benefits of a health and fitness program. To me, there is nothing better than the feeling of exhaustion after a workout, to make you feel like you really did a good job, and did something for yourself. I specialize in weight loss programs, and help clients reach their goals through various techniques including circuit training, HIIT, bootcamps, as well as strength and functional training exercises.

  • WHAT'S INCLUDED
  • Wether you want to lose weight or build strength, your body needs to be able to perform the daily activities in an efficient way. We create functional training programs that will lead to better joint mobility and stability, as well as more efficient motor patterns. Improving these factors decreases the potential for an injury sustained during athletic exercises. The benefits may arise from the use of training that emphasizes the body's natural ability to move in six degrees of freedom.
